Policy.

How we collect, utilize, and protect your digital footprint when you work with us.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with JW Digital—whether by submitting an inquiry, logging into the Client Portal, or making a direct purchase—we collect specific information to facilitate our work. This includes your name, email address, phone number, business details, and any branding assets you upload.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for operational purposes:

  • To fulfill your purchased services and deliver your website or branding assets.
  • To communicate regarding project timelines, revisions, and feedback.
  • To process secure payments through our trusted third-party processors.

JW Digital does not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize industry-standard, secure third-party processors to run our agency. By using our site, you acknowledge that your data may be processed by:

  • Stripe: For all secure checkout and payment processing. We do not directly store your credit card data.
  • Formspree: To securely route your project inquiries to our inbox.
  • Netlify & SteinHQ: To host our web infrastructure and operate our secure Client Portal.

4. Data Security

We implement standard digital security measures to protect your information and project assets. However, no digital transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You use our digital forms and portal at your own risk. We strongly recommend using services like 1Password or LastPass when transmitting sensitive login credentials for your existing domains or hosting platforms.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about how we handle your data, you can reach out directly via our Contact Page or by emailing us at jwdigitaltx@gmail.com.
