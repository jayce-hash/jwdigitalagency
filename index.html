About

Built on
Principle.

JW Digital is run by Jayce — a marketing graduate from Liberty University and founder of Concerto, a live music discovery platform on iOS and Android. The agency came from a problem he kept running into, not a business plan.

Good businesses were invisible online. Not because the work was bad — because the image was. Owners who'd invested everything into what they built were losing customers daily to competitors whose only advantage was a better website. The work deserved better.

JW Digital exists to close that gap — with the same level of craft and attention a $500,000 engagement gets, at rates that don't require a Series A to justify. One person. Direct communication. Work that holds up.
