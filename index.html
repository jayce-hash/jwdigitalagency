About

One Person.
No Markup.

JW Digital is run by Jayce, a marketing graduate from Liberty University and founder of Concerto, a live music app available on iOS and Android.

The agency side came out of a simple observation: most local businesses in DFW are losing customers every day to a bad website or a dead Instagram, and the agencies charging $5,000 to fix it aren’t in any hurry to make it affordable.

JW Digital exists to close that gap. Flat rates, fast turnaround, and you deal with one person who actually cares whether it works.
