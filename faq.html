Knowledge Base

Common
Questions.

Everything you need to know about how we work, what we build, and what you get.

What platforms do you build websites on?

We are platform-agnostic, meaning we choose the tool that fits your business. Most of our high-performance marketing sites are built using Webflow or custom HTML/CSS for lightning-fast load times. For e-commerce, we primarily use Shopify.

What if I don't like the first draft?

That's what the revision rounds are for! We include structural revision rounds with every web build and branding package. We refine the work together until the aesthetic and function match your vision.

Do I have to pay for hosting?

Yes. JW Digital charges a flat-rate for the design and build of your site, but standard domain registration and website hosting (e.g., Webflow hosting, Shopify plans) are billed directly to your credit card by those platforms. We will help you set these accounts up during handover so you have 100% ownership.

How long does a website build take?

Unlike traditional agencies that drag projects out for months, we move fast. A standard 1-to-3 page website build typically takes a few days to a week from the moment we receive your assets. Larger builds may take 2-3 weeks. We will confirm your specific timeline within 48 hours of purchase.

Why don't you do discovery calls?

We respect your time. Traditional discovery calls often end up being hour-long sales pitches. We prefer you to review our transparent pricing, make a direct purchase, and fill out our intake form so we can immediately get to work building your business.
